Biography

I received the Ph.D. degrees from Fudan University (FDU), Shanghai, China, in 2020. I am currently a lecturer of the School of Economics and Finance for Shanghai International Studies University (SISU), with a joint appointment at the Big data Financial Research Center. I also got Business Partner from two companies, ROG TECHNOLOGY and 壹零零壹未来科技有限公司. My research focuses on graph learning (especially Graph Convolutional Neural Network,GCN), recommendation, and financial and medical data analysis.
